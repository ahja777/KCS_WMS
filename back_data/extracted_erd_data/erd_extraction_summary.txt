--- HILO ERD Entity Summary by Category ---

  [데이터권한관리] Data Authority Management - User permissions, carrier/owner/service mappings
    - ILUSER (사용자정보)
    - ILCARRIER (실행사)
    - ILTRANSSERVICE (운송서비스)
    - ILORGANIZATION (사업부)
    - ILOWNER (화주)
    - 운송서비스사업부매핑
    - 운송서비스사용자매핑
    - 운송서비스실행사매핑
    - 운송서비스화주매핑

  [마스터] Master Data - Transport patterns, vehicles, drivers, routes, items, customers, tariffs
    - ILTRANSPATTERN (운송패턴)
    - ILTRANSSERVICE (운송서비스)
    - ILORGANIZATION (사업부)
    - ILOWNER (화주)
    - ILDEPOT (센터)
    - ILOWNERTODEPOTLINK (화주사관리센터)
    - ILCUSTOMER (거래처)
    - ILAREATOCUSTOMERLINK (거래처권역매핑)
    - ILITEMGROUP (제품그룹)
    - ILITEMCLASS (제품분류)
    - ILITEM (제품)
    - ILCARRIER (실행사)
    - ILCARRIERTOOWNERLINK
    - ILVEHICLE (차량)
    - ILVEHICLECONTRACT (차량계약정보)
    - ILTARIFFBYVEHICLETYPE (차량타입별계약단가)
    - ILDRIVER (운전자정보)
    - ILDRIVERSCHEDULE (운전자스케줄)
    - ILVEHICLESCHEDULE (차량스케줄)
    - ILEQUIPMENT (차량장비)
    - ILEQUIPMENTTYPE (차량장비타입)
    - ILDEFINE (사용자정의정보)
    - ILROUTE (운송루트)
    - ILROUTELINE (운송루트세부)
    - ILCONVERSION (도량형환산)
    - ILUSERTOOWNERLINK (사용자별화주권한)
    - ILUSERTODEPOTLINK (사용자별센터권한)
    - ILUSERTOCARRIERLINK (사용자별실행사권한)
    - ILTRANSLINEINFODEFINE (원운송오더세부특화정보정의)
    - ILTRANSINFODEFINE (원오더헤더특화정보정의)
    - ILZIPCODE (우편번호)
    - ILITEMPRICE (제품가격)
    - ILPACK (운송포장)
    - ILCARRIERSELECTIONRULE (실행사배차규칙)
    - ILTARIFF (운송비)
    - ILEFFECTIVETARIFF (운송비유효기간)
    - ILCHARGECODE (정산코드)
    - ILAREA (권역)

  [시스템] System - Menus, users, groups, programs, configs, logs, boards
    - ILBOARD (게시판)
    - ILBOARDCOMMENT (게시판댓글)
    - ILNSTR (메시지)
    - ILMENU (메뉴)
    - ILGROUP (사용자그룹)
    - ILUSERTOGROUPLINK (사용자그룹매핑)
    - ILGROUPTOPROGRAMLINK (그룹프로그램매핑)
    - ILUSER (사용자)
    - ILUSERTOPROGRAMLINK (사용자프로그램매핑)
    - ILCONFIG (설정)
    - ILSEQUENCE (시퀀스)
    - ILASSEMBLY (어셈블리)
    - ILACTIVEUSER (활성사용자)
    - ILNOTICE (공지사항)
    - ILLOG (로그)
    - ILPATH (연계경로)
    - ILPROGRAM (프로그램)

  [오더배차] Order/Dispatch - Transport orders, schedules, turns, inventory, receipts, tasks, GPS
    - ILRESOURCE (리소스/배차)
    - ILORGTRANSORDER (원오더)
    - ILORGTRANSORDERLINE (원오더라인)
    - ILTRANSFORECAST (조달오더)
    - ILTRANSINFO (운송특화정보)
    - ILTRANSLINEINFO (운송특화세부정보)
    - ILTRANSORDER (운송오더)
    - ILTRANSORDERLINE (운송오더라인)
    - ILSCHEDULE (스케줄)
    - ILSCHEDULETOORDERLINK (스케줄오더매핑)
    - ILTURN (턴)
    - ILTRANSCOST (운송비)
    - ILTRANSCOSTBYORDERLINE (오더라인별운송비)
    - ILCONTENT (입고예정정보)
    - ILINVENTORY (재고)
    - ILINVENTORYBYDATE (일별재고)
    - ILINVENTORYBYMONTH (월별재고)
    - ILRECEIPT (출고예정정보)
    - ILRECEIPTLINE (출고예정세부)
    - ILTASK (작업)
    - ILORDERDISPATCHRESULT (배차결과)
    - ILGPSTRACE (GPS추적)
    - ILCLAIM (클레임)

  [정산] Settlement - Revenue/expense aggregation, tax invoices
    - 기간별매출집계 (ILCHARGEBYOWNERBYMONTH)
    - 세부항목별매출집계 (ILCHARGEDETAILBYMONTH)
    - 기간별매입집계 (ILPAYBYCARRIERBYMONTH)
    - 세부항목별매입집계 (ILPAYDETAILBYMONTH)
    - 매출세금계산서 (ILSALESINVOICE)
    - 매출세금계산서세부 (ILSALESINVOICELINE)
    - 매입세금계산서 (ILPURCHASEINVOICE)
    - 매입세금계산서세부 (ILPURCHASEINVOICELINE)
